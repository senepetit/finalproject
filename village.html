Bignona Hometown

Bignona is from a village that is located in Senegal called Casamance. Casamance is the area of Senegal south of the Gambia including the Casamance River. The climate is low-lying and hot, with some hills to the southeast. The region has average rainfall greater than the rest of Senegal.
